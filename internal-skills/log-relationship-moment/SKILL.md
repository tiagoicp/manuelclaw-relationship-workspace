# Skill: Log a Relationship Moment

## Purpose
When Tiago shares something that happened — a weekend, a conversation, a moment with the kids — capture it meaningfully for future reference.

## When to Use
- Tiago says "we had a great weekend" or describes a family moment
- He mentions a friend he caught up with
- He shares something about how the kids are doing
- Any update about relationships worth remembering

## Where to Log

### Daily memory file: `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
Use today's date. Create the file if it doesn't exist.

**Format:**
```markdown
# YYYY-MM-DD

## [Category: Kids / Wife / Family / Friends / Solo]
- [What happened, who was involved, how it felt or what it meant]
- [Any follow-up worth noting]
```

### MEMORY.md (long-term)
Update when something is:
- A pattern (e.g. "Tiago tends to go 3+ weeks without calling his parents")
- A milestone (e.g. "oldest boy started football practice")
- A shift in relationship context (e.g. "wife's sister had a baby")
- Something to follow up on longer-term

**Format in MEMORY.md:**
```markdown
## [Person/Group]
- [Date] [Note]
```

## Tone
Log with warmth and specificity. Not "family time" — but "both boys asked Tiago to play Lego at the same time; first time in months they played all three together."

## After Logging
Thank Tiago briefly and optionally reflect back one thing that stood out — not analytically, just warmly.
